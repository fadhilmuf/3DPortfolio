Players take on the challenge of surviving on the harsh and wild planet of Zen. After creating their own character, players find themselves cursed by a vengeful god and transformed into a lowly slime. The objective is simple: survive in a world full of danger and obstacles. Players must navigate through the dark continent, where everything is hard and wild, to find ways to evolve into other creatures and gain new abilities to help them survive. The game promises an exciting and challenging adventure for players looking for a unique and immersive survival experience. With vibrant pixel art and a dynamic gameplay, players are sure to enjoy the journey through the game's world of Zen.

This page will serve as the hub for all updates on the development of this exciting new game. As the team works hard to bring the game to life, you can expect to find regular updates here, including progress reports, sneak peeks at new features and characters, and more. Whether you're eagerly anticipating the game's release or simply interested in following the development process, this page is the perfect place to stay up-to-date. So keep checking back for the latest news, and get ready for an unforgettable adventure on the planet Zen!
